Interior Plantscape

Indoor environments can gain from incorporating natural elements such as plants and biophilic design, leading to reduced stress, increased productivity, and improved well-being. Spaces influenced by nature in schools, workplaces, and healthcare settings result in greater satisfaction and performance. Design that connects with nature encourages a healthier and more joyful way of living.

Container Plantings

Each container at Petals + Bloom is uniquely crafted to elevate your environment. We enjoy experimenting with color and texture to showcase a diverse range of plants. Similar to how an artist blends colors to add depth and dimension, we use various plants to create a vibrant palette for your enjoyment. Allow us to design seasonal rotations for SPRING, SUMMER, FALL & WINTER, transforming your favorite spots – whether it's your backyard, front porch, or business entrance – into serene retreats. Navigating through endless aisles of options can be overwhelming and stressful. Let us ease your burden by selecting plants that thrive in Colorado's changing seasons. This way, you, your friends, family, and colleagues can unwind and connect with nature without worry.

Landscape Design

Dreaming of HGTV coming to your yard? Petals + Bloom loves to bring your ideas to life. From patio’s to flower beds we have you covered. Whether you're envisioning a cozy outdoor retreat with a charming fire pit and comfortable seating, or a vibrant garden bursting with colorful blooms and lush greenery, our team is dedicated to crafting spaces that reflect your style and enhance your outdoor living experience. With our expertise and passion for design, we can transform any ordinary yard into a stunning sanctuary that you'll enjoy for years to come. Let us make your landscape dreams a reality!
