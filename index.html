We are Petals & Bloom Designs.

Excited about incorporating nature into your indoor spaces? We are a small business based in Northern Colorado. We specialize in creating warm and inviting environments that seamlessly blend the indoors with the outdoors. Whether it's for your office or home, we are eager to design a space you will love!

Our Process

1
Consultation
During our consultation process, we'll discuss your vision, assess your space and needs, and review your project budget.
2
Design
Next, we'll create a stunning design plan that perfectly compliments your space. We'll talk plant selection, lighting requirements, pairings, placements, and layout.
3
Installation & Beyond
From delivery to install, we'll handle everything to bring your vision to life, including ongoing maintenence to keep your plantscape alive and thriving for years to come.

Outdoor Living Spaces

Extend your living spaces outside. Whether it's an inviting patio, a vibrant garden, or a elegant lounge area, we love creating stunning outdoor plantscapes that transform your space into an inviting oasis. From lush greenery and colorful flowers to elegant planters and thoughtful landscaping, the right design can elevate your outdoor space, adding the perfect touch of beauty and nature to your home or office.

According to a recent study, designing plants into your surroundings has a strong corelation with lower stress levels and improved productivity.

Biophilic design principles are recognized across industries as an important lifestyle mechanism, fostering productivity, mindfulness, sleep quality, and even immunity.

Plants have also been shown to improve indoor air quality, having a positive impact on our physical and mental health, reducing the likelihood of concerns such as sad building syndrome
